Bitcoin Waterfall Chart & Altcoin Market Cap Sequencing Function
Overview
The Bitcoin Waterfall Chart & Altcoin Sequencing function is an advanced analytical tool designed to visualize, predict, and optimize the flow of Bitcoin's market dominance into selected altcoins. By leveraging both historical and real-time data, this feature identifies the most probable sequences and altcoins that gain market capitalization as Bitcoin dominance declines. It empowers users to strategically invest in altcoins, ladder their gains, and maximize returns within the top 200 cryptocurrencies, including medium and small caps.
